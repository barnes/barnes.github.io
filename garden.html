I want to be better at growing my own veggies. I have a nice yard with plenty of sunlight, so why not? That being said, I have no green thumb and its been hard. This page will highlight some of trials and tribulations to becoming a backyard farmer.

I have taken to Square Foot Gardening, mostly because I needed a very prescriptive approach to gardent. "Do precisely this, and you'll have good results." Well, as luck would have it, the first season of the boxes was pretty successful. Plenty of hiccups, but largely great. This season, I can start to question 'why did Mel tell me to do this?' and start to develop my own techniques. This season I'll be operating 4 beds, 2 of which are 4'x2', and 2 of which are 2'x5'.
