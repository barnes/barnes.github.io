Who am I?

I am a former middle school teacher turned micro-electronic process engineering, obsessive learner, jack of many trades, master of none. Currently I'm taking on the task of learning modern web development, brushing off the long forgotten days of doing work on Wordpress themes and creating custom Drupal installations. I have ambitions to build simple data collection and analysis tools for personal projects, for things like tracking garden growth and monitoring cycling performance. When I'm not working on projects, I'm probably with Daisy, the best do in the world.
